Quick Start Tips

  • Safety First: Always follow range rules and never point a bow at anyone
  • Start Light: Begin with a lower draw weight to develop proper form
  • Get Instruction: Consider taking lessons from a certified archery instructor
  • Practice Regularly: Consistent practice builds muscle memory and accuracy
  • Be Patient: Archery skills develop over time - focus on form over distance initially
